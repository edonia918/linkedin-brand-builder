Common Questions

Everything you need to know

Do I need writing experience?

Not at all. LaunchBrand is specifically built for people who don't consider themselves writers. You provide the raw material - the AI does the writing.

What if my experience feels basic?

That's exactly who this is designed for. Class projects, part time jobs, volunteer work, and internships are all story worthy experiences. You just need the right framing.

How many posts can I generate?

The free demo lets you generate unlimited sample posts. Full access includes a complete 30 day content calendar and profile optimization tools.

Is this just a generic writing AI?

No. It's specifically trained on high performing LinkedIn content patterns for early career professionals. Generic writing tools don't account for LinkedIn's algorithm, engagement signals, or recruiter behavior.
