My Story

From invisible online to
getting noticed by recruiters

When I first graduated, I had an internship at a local firm, two class projects I was genuinely proud of, and a LinkedIn profile that hadn't been updated in three years. I knew I needed a presence online, but every time I sat down to write a post, I froze.

"What would I even say? Who would care? I wasn't a thought leader - I was a new grad trying to get a job."

After months of trial and error, I cracked the code. The secret to LinkedIn wasn't sounding impressive. It was learning how to frame ordinary experiences in a way that showed self awareness, growth, and genuine curiosity.

So I built LaunchBrand - a tool that helps people just like I was take the experiences they already have and turn them into content that actually builds a career.

What I Believe

Three principles behind everything I build

🌱

Everyone Starts Somewhere

No one is born knowing how to build a personal brand. The tools and guidance you need to get started should be accessible, approachable, and built for beginners - not polished professionals.

🎯

Specificity Beats Generality

Generic writing tools will give you a generic post. LinkedIn success for early career professionals requires specific insights. What hooks work, what industries respond to, and what recruiters actually look for.

🔑

Your Story Is Already There

You don't need more experience to start building your brand. You need a better lens for the experience you already have. That's exactly what LaunchBrand helps you find.
